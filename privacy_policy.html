File Time Lock Privacy Policy

At File Time Lock, your privacy is a top priority. We are committed to safeguarding the confidentiality and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle and protect data related to your use of our application.

Data Collection

File Time Lock does not collect any personal or sensitive data from its users. We do not gather information such as names, email addresses, location data, or any other personal identifiers.

File Access and Locking

Our app's sole purpose is to enable the locking and unlocking of files based on your preferences. The files you choose to lock are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party entities.

Internet Time Sync

File Time Lock may access the internet to synchronize time accurately, ensuring that locked files are unlocked only after the designated time period has passed. This process does not involve the collection of any personal information.

Analytics and Tracking

We do not utilize any analytics or tracking mechanisms within our app. Your usage patterns, interactions, and any other actions within the app remain solely on your device and are not shared with us or any external parties.

Permissions

File Time Lock requires minimal permissions to function effectively, such as access to your device's storage to lock and unlock files. These permissions are solely for the purpose of providing you with the intended functionality of the app and are not used for any other purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any data from our users, as we do not collect any personal or user-related information. Your files and interactions within the app remain completely private and within the confines of your device.

Updates and Changes
